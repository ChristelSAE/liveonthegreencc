LIVE ON THE GREEN featuring Animal Ventura

‍JOIN US on campus or online for our platform of excellent student collaborations in displaying talented musicians with the help of SAE professional trainers and mentors from the audio & music industry.

‍Every trimester, SAE presents LIVE ON THE GREEN, a music event live on campus and streamed world wide, featuring local talent.

The goal of this collaboration is for students to experience hands-on industry level production with FRESH ideations and have fun in the process. The Goal of this event is to re-FRESH the target audiences, primarily SAE students who have done their best in their study along the trimester.
